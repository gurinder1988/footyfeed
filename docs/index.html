About FootyFeed

FootyFeed brings your favorite football content together in one place. Stay updated with the latest news, watch highlights, and listen to podcasts from your preferred teams.

Key Features:

  • Personalized feed based on your favorite team
  • Latest football news and updates
  • Match highlights and video content
  • Curated football podcasts
  • Clean, intuitive interface
  • Real-time content updates

Privacy Policy

FootyFeed respects your privacy and does not collect any personal information. The app only stores your favorite team preference locally on your device to provide personalized content.

All content is fetched from public APIs and sources. We do not track your usage or share any data with third parties.
