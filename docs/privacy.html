Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

FootyFeed is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

FootyFeed is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Your favorite team preference (stored locally on your device)
  • App usage statistics (anonymous, through App Store Analytics)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide personalized content based on your favorite team
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage

All personal preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

FootyFeed uses the following third-party services:

  • YouTube API for video content
  • RSS feeds for news content
  • Podcast RSS feeds for audio content

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service.
